How to Add Knovon for Kids to Your Home Screen

To use this app, please add it to your home screen:

  1. Open the browser menu (usually three dots or a share icon).
  2. Select "Add to Home Screen".
  3. Follow the prompts to install the app.
  4. Reopen the app from your home screen.
